About Property

Overview

Country Inn & Suites by Radisson, Candolim Goa is a great choice for travellers looking for a 5 star hotel in Goa. It is located in Candolim. This Hotel stands out as one of the highly recommended hotel in Goa and is recommended by 91% of our guests. Hotel is rated 4.1 out of 5, which is considered as Very good. Some of the popular transit points from the hotel are Karmali Railway Station (16.5 kms), Majorda Railway Station (27.2 kms), Kulem Railway Station (54.3 kms) and Dabolim Airport (16.0 kms). The Hotel is in proximity to some popular tourist attractions and other places of interest in Goa. Some of the tourist attractions include Valanka Shopping Mall (3.2 kms), Caculo Mall (5.6 kms), Mall De Goa (6.2 kms), Morjim Beach (13.1 kms), Church of St Cajetan (15.4 kms), Mandrem Beach (17.5 kms) and Arambol Beach (20.7 kms).

From all the 5 Star hotels in Goa, Country Inn & Suites by Radisson, Candolim Goa is very much popular among the tourists. A smooth check-in/check-out process, flexible policies and friendly management garner great customer satisfaction for this property. The Hotel has standard Check-In time as 02:00 PM and Check-Out time as 11:00 AM. It is a couple-friendly property, hence it is absolutely safe for unmarried couples to stay here.

Hotel & Room Amenities

All the amenities and services of Country Inn & Suites by Radisson, Candolim Goa focus on the convenience of its guests. The hotel offers a well-equipped meeting/conference room with video conferencing facilities, a spacious banquet hall for hosting personal parties and social gatherings. The Hotel also offers a dedicated parking area. The Hotel also boasts of a beautiful swimming pool. The fitness centre/gym of the hotel can also be accessed by the guests. The in-house restaurant of the hotel offers a wide range of cuisines. This Hotel also has a Spa which makes the overall stay experience even more comforting.

This property offers a wide range of room categories such as Suite-Living Room-Pool view, SUPERIOR ROOM WITH AIR CONDITIONER, Superior Room-Garden Facing, Deluxe Room-Pool view and few more along with different meal plan options to choose from. With room facilities like Wheelchair, Ironing Service, TV, Balcony/Terrace and 24-hour Room Service, the property provides a comfortable stay experience.

Hotel Policies

According to government regulations, a valid Photo ID has to be carried by every person above the age of 18 staying at Country Inn & Suites by Radisson, Candolim Goa. The identification proofs accepted are Drivers License, Voters Card, Passport, Ration Card. Without valid ID the guest will not be allowed to check in.

The primary guest checking in to the hotel must be at least 18 years of age.

The standard check-in time is 1400 and the standard check-out time is 1100. After booking you will be sent an email confirmation with hotel phone number. You can contact the hotel directly for early check-in or late check-out. Early check-in or late check-out is subject to availability and may be chargeable by Country Inn & Suites by Radisson, Candolim Goa.

This is a couple friendly property. Unmarried/Unrelated couples are allowed to check-in. Local Ids can be presented as proof of identification. Final rights of admission/check-in remain reserved with the hotel management & refund can be denied in-case any misconduct is observed by the hotel management.

The room tariff includes all taxes. The amount paid for the room does not include charges for optional services and facilities (such as room service, mini bar, snacks or telephone calls). These will be charged by the hotel at the time of check-out from the hotel.
